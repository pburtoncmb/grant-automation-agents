REQUEST FOR PROPOSALS: Community Health Initiative Grant

OVERVIEW:
The Foundation is seeking proposals for innovative community health programs. 
Applicants must demonstrate a track record of success in health service delivery.

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS:
- Organizations must be 501(c)(3) nonprofits
- Applicants must have at least 3 years of operational history
- Program must serve underrepresented communities

IMPORTANT DATES:
- Letter of Intent Deadline: September 15, 2025
- Application Deadline: October 30, 2025
- Award Notification: December 15, 2025

BUDGET INFORMATION:
Grant amounts range from $50,000 to $250,000. Applicants shall provide detailed budget justification.

SCORING CRITERIA:
1. Innovation (25 points): Applicants should propose novel approaches to addressing health disparities.
2. Impact (40 points): Programs must demonstrate measurable outcomes.
3. Sustainability (20 points): Organizations should describe funding plans beyond the grant period.
4. Capacity (15 points): Applicants are required to show adequate staffing and infrastructure.
